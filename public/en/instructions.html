Welcome to the Prisoner's Dilemma (Demo)

Powered by nodeGame.

In each round, both players must make the choice to cooperate or defect. If both players choose to collaborate, they each receive $1. If both players defect, they each receive $0.30. If one choose to cooperate and the other doesn't, the cooperator receives $0 while the defector receives $1.6. The game is repeated 3 rounds.
